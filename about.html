About GestureQuiz

GestureQuiz is an innovative educational platform that combines traditional learning with modern gesture-based interaction. Based in Qatar, we're revolutionizing the way students learn and teachers teach by making education more engaging, interactive, and memorable.

Our platform allows educators to create interactive quizzes where students respond using hand gestures, making learning a multi-sensory experience that improves retention and engagement.

Our Mission

To make education more accessible, engaging, and effective through innovative technology that adapts to different learning styles, serving the educational community in Qatar and beyond.

Whether you're a teacher looking to engage your students in new ways or a student wanting a more interactive learning experience, GestureQuiz provides the tools you need to succeed.

Join thousands of educators and students who are already transforming their learning experience with GestureQuiz.
